Research

I'm interested in machine learning and edge computing. Much of my research is about efficient inference of machine learning models on edge devices. It includes low latency and high throughput with negligible effect on accuracy.

Deep learning is getting popular to a wide range of applications such as image classification, detection, machine translation, self-driving cars, Smart Robots and AlphaGo. In these applications, high accuracy is the primary goal. In recent trend, it has been observed that demand of high accuracy leads to big complex models. These large models ensure high accuracy but they create following problems and challenges

Deployment/update problem

Large training time

More memory access, high energy, high latency and low throughput

My research focus is to:

  • study different model optimizations and the use of tensor cores for reduced precision and efficient use of memory.

  • implement TensorRT, TF-TRT and grapple frameworks to observe optimization effect on deep learning models.

  • use different profilers and gpu monitoring softwares such as Nsight tools, tensorboard profilers, nvprof and nvtop.

  • optimize state of an art models like transformers for the edge devices.
